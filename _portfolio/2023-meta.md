- Trained and integrated **CNN-based vision models** enabling [haptic glove tracking for VR](https://tech.facebook.com/reality-labs/2021/11/inside-reality-labs-meet-the-team-thats-bringing-touch-to-the-digital-world/), improving tracking accuracy under severe occlusions with reduced error of +50% over the previous solution.
- Developed multimodal signal modeling pipelines integrating multiple fisheye lens images and diverse sensor inputs (e.g., mocap, tactile, IMU, optical fiber) into synchronized, display-ready formats to inform sensor hardware strategy.
- Eliminated hundreds of hours on manual review by buliding an automatic image data filtration framework that enhanced dataset quality and the downstream model performance.
- Accelerated iteration cycles by 3× through designing and deploying a multi-GPU training pipeline with automation.
